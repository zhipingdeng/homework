# 制作html5游戏

## 开始

设置,启动construct2。单击文件按钮,并选择“NEW”

![fdgh](https://www.scirra.com/images/articles/filenew.png)

弹出对话框

![fdgh](https://www.scirra.com/images/articles/newprojdialog65.png)

选择“New empty project”
